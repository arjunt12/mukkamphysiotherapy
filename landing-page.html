Our Physiotherapy Services at Mukkam

At our Mukkam center, we provide a comprehensive range of physiotherapy services to address various conditions and promote overall well-being.

Orthopedic Rehab

Effective treatment for back pain, knee pain, shoulder injuries, arthritis, and post-operative recovery.

Neurological Physio

Specialized care for stroke rehabilitation, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and balance disorders.

Sports Injury Rehab

Expert care for athletes to recover from injuries, prevent recurrence, and enhance performance.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Dedicated programs for seniors to improve mobility, reduce fall risk, and maintain independence.

Pain Management

Holistic approaches including manual therapy, electrotherapy, and exercise to alleviate chronic pain.
